SD-WAN & SASE

Traditional WAN architectures were not built for cloud applications, distributed workforces, or zero-trust security. SD-WAN and SASE give you the performance, visibility, and security your modern network demands — at a fraction of the cost of legacy MPLS.

SD-WAN & SASE

The Modern WAN Your Business Needs

If your network was designed before cloud applications became the backbone of your business, it's costing you in performance, security, and dollars. Cirrux Networks helps organizations transition to software-defined WAN and secure access service edge architectures that are built for today's hybrid, multi-cloud environment.

📡

SD-WAN Design & Deployment

We evaluate your application landscape, traffic patterns, and branch locations to design an SD-WAN architecture that delivers consistent performance across all sites.

🔒

SASE Implementation

Converge networking and security into a single cloud-delivered platform — combining SD-WAN with ZTNA, CASB, SWG, and FWaaS for comprehensive protection at the edge.

📊

Application Performance

Intelligent traffic steering prioritizes business-critical applications like Microsoft 365, Salesforce, and UCaaS — ensuring consistent user experience regardless of transport.

💰

WAN Cost Optimization

Replace expensive MPLS circuits with a hybrid of broadband, LTE, and DIA — maintaining reliability and performance while dramatically reducing connectivity spend.
